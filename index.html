Meet Our Founder: Pastor Ehijie Oriabure

Lead Pastor, GWC.

"The mandate is simple: to transform followers into and efficient leaders for God's Kingdom."

The vision for Grace Word Center was received by our founder, Pastor Ehijie, in the city of Port Harcourt. His passion is rooted in the divine instruction to raise individuals who are empowered both spiritually and practically.

From humble beginnings, Pastor Ehijie, has faithfully led this spiritual family, focusing on deep word studies and practical application, ensuring every member is equipped to fulfil their purpose.

Our Vision

The vision of Grace Word Center is to raise a global network of believers who are empowered by the Word and the Spirit to function as effective and efficient leaders in all spheres of life, thereby advancing the Kingdom of God.

We are dedicated to building faith, fostering spiritual growth, and impacting our community and beyond with the transforming power of the Gospel.

  • Grace Word Worship: Leading congregants into deeper communion with God through music.
  • Grace Word Teens: Building faith foundations for the next generation of leaders.
  • The Watch Intercessory Team: Dedicated prayer and spiritual warfare unit.
  • Welfare and Outreach: Serving the local community and providing support to those in need.

We believe every member has a place to serve and grow.
